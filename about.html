  A Better Camera Store was founded in 2024 when the founder, Jasper Robinson took a dream and made it a reality. He became interested in photography in 2022 when he bought an old film camera online. He fell in love with the art of photography and the magical experience of film photography. That camera sparked an interest and Jasper learned everything he could about the art.

  He eventually applied to work at a local camera store in the hope that he could learn as much as possible and have fun doing it. He learned to really love the experience of helping customers with their photography needs and new he wanted to continue. He had plenty of ideas to improve the store he worked at, but the owners didn't have a passion for it enough to make a change. He decided to open his own camera store where the focus was on the customers and their needs and the services could be completed efficiently with that in mind.

  He found that St. George didn't have a camera store and saw a need that he could fill. He and his wife, Bella decided to take a chance and chase this dream. A Better Camera Store Company was born and Jasper's Camera opened in 2024!
